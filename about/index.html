Our story

Rooted in Naples. Built on trust.

CBX is a family-owned company led by a Naples native with nearly 15 years of hands-on tree-care experience.

Meet Giovani

Born and raised in Naples, Giovani has spent his career working with the trees, palms, weather, and properties that make Southwest Florida unique.

He founded CBX Tree Service to provide the kind of service homeowners often struggle to find: direct communication, practical advice, honest pricing, fast scheduling, and a crew that cares about the finished property—not only the cut.

Giovani is hands-on with estimates and projects from start to finish. That owner involvement gives customers a consistent point of contact and helps ensure every detail is understood.
